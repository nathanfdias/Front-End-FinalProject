WHO'S THE STRONGEST AVENGER?

Doctor Strange Wins

Although Doctor Strange fails to defeat the Scarlet Witch, he proves that his power and skill in magic are just two assets that make him the strongest Avenger. He defends himself against Wanda in her catastrophic attack on Kamar-Taj, overwhelming her at one point with fierce Eldritch magic in the form of snakes. Doctor Strange more than proved his strength when he fought Thanos on Titan. Though Thanos possessed several Infinity Stones, Strange held back the Mad Titan. The two face off after the Avengers and Guardians of the Galaxy fail to take the Infinity Gauntlet from Thanos, and Strange nearly wins the day. Thanos created a singularity with the Space Stone, threatening to suck everything into it, including Strange. Strange dispersed the singularity into butterflies with a wave of his hand.
